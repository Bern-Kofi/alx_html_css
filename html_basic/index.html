This is Saint Joseph's National School. The school was founded by Mr. Appaih Joseph. He also taught in the school. The school is located in Pankrono, Kumasi in the Ashanti Region of Ghana.The school has two main divisions, the Junior High School and the Primary School. Each class has a class teacher. The school also has professional and qualified teachers.
