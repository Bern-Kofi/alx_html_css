This is Saint Joseph's National School. The school was founded by Mr. Appaih Joseph. He also taught in the school. The school is located in Pankrono, Kumasi in the Ashanti Region of Ghana.The school has two main divisions, the Junior High School and the Primary School. Each class has a class teacher. The school also has professional and qualified teachers.


The School Building

Junior High School (JHS)

The high school consist of three(3) classes, Junior High School 1,Junior High School 2 and Junior High School 3.The short form is J.H.S 1,2 and 3.The list is below the paragraph is the llink to their respective webpages. The JHS teach the following subjects as electives, Religious and Moral Education (R.M.E),Basic Design and Technology (B.D.T), Information and Communication and Technology (I.C.T) Ghanaian Languge (Ashanti Twi) and French.
